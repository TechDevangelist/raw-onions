*******************
**** Cartridge ****
*******************

Cartridge simulate a game cartridge inserted into your handheld, for fastest boots and shutdowns.

For those who plan to do several sessions on the same game, it is time saving and user friendly.

By Totofaki.
Special thanks to Shaun Inman, Eggs, Johny on Flame.

Cartridge icon : Creatype
Onion avatar png : Tim Swast

PS: This app had been thoroughly tested, but in case of an unwanted behaviour, just remove the .tmp_update folder in your Sd Card.
